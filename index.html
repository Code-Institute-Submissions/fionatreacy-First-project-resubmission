Newgrange

An ancient monument built by Irelands neolithic people


An Exceptional Relic From a Forgotten Time

Ancient


Built approximately 5200 years ago, Newgrange is a relic of teh Neolithic era and predates both the Great Pyramids of Giza and Stonehenge

Cultural


Newgrange is believed to have been of significant importance to the people who built it, being used as a tomb for the dead and as a temple of worship

Grand


At 85 meters in diameter and 12 meters high, Newgrange covers an area of over one acre with over 200,000 tonnes of rock being used in its construction
